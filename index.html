Privacy Policy

BK Kidney Care ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal and medical information of its users.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information to provide medical and app-related services:

  • Basic account information (such as name, email address, phone number)
  • Health-related information provided by the user
  • Images or documents uploaded by the user (such as medical reports)
  • App usage and interaction data

Camera Permission

The app uses the device camera only to allow users to capture and upload images or documents required for medical or app-related functionality. Camera data is used only with user consent.

How We Use Your Data

User data is used only for providing app features such as medical services, consultations, communication, and account management. We do not sell user data.

Data Sharing

We do not share personal or medical data with third parties, except when required by law or to provide essential app services.

Data Security

All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using secure communication protocols (HTTPS/TLS). We take reasonable measures to protect user data.

Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

Account and Data Deletion

Users can request deletion of their account and associated personal and medical data at any time.

To request account deletion, please send an email to:
doctor@bkarogyam.com

Upon verification, we will delete the account and associated data within 30 days, except data that must be retained for legal or regulatory purposes.

Children’s Privacy

BK Kidney Care is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
