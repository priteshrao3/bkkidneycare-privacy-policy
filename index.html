Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 23 February 2026

BK Kidney Care ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by B.K. Arogyam & Research Pvt. Ltd. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal and medical information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use the BK Kidney Care mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

A. Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number

B. Health & Medical Information

  • Medical history provided by user
  • Medical reports and lab documents
  • Images captured or uploaded through the app
  • Consultation-related data

C. Technical Information

  • Device type and OS version
  • App usage logs
  • IP address
  • Crash reports

2. Camera Permission

The app requests camera permission solely to allow users to capture medical reports and upload required health documents.

Camera access is only activated when the user chooses to use this feature. We do not access the camera in the background.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide medical consultation services
  • Manage user accounts
  • Facilitate communication
  • Improve app functionality
  • Ensure security and fraud prevention

We do not sell or rent personal or medical data to third parties.

4. Data Sharing

  • With authorized medical professionals for consultation
  • With service providers required to operate the app (such as cloud hosting)
  • When required by law

All third parties are bound by confidentiality obligations.

5. Data Security

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Access control restrictions

However, no system is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal and medical data as long as the account remains active or as required by applicable laws and regulations.

7. Account and Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.

To request deletion, please email:

📧 doctor@bkarogyam.com

Upon verification, we will delete user data within 30 days, except information required for legal compliance.

8. Children's Privacy

This application is intended only for users aged 18 years and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

9. Medical Disclaimer

BK Kidney Care provides health-related information and consultation services. It does not replace emergency medical services.

Users should consult licensed medical professionals for medical emergencies.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
